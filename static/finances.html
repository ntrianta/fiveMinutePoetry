Finances
The bird sat on the branch.
After a month he could not make rent so he had to fly out.

The dog stopped to pee on the telephone pole.
He got a fine so big that he had to work an extra shift and broke up with his lover.

The snake tried to bite the little girl.
But his left tooth was hurting for a week now but had no money to go check it up so he gave it another thought.

The bee was flying around on a warm Monday morning.
But ran out of gas and did not fly again until she got a job.

The shark swam to the coast.
But then he felt terror because he realized he couldn't find his way home since his GPS was stolen and he could not afford a new one.

Jade was nervous because she wouldn't probably be able to pay her daughter's tuition this month.
For an instant she thought how happy she would be if she was an animal and did not have to think about money.

Maybe in your next life Jade!
