I was born and then art pressed 'pause' and waited for me. I would not call my self a poet. Just an earthling. I write using my heart and sometimes a Macbook pro. I listen to the sounds of earth and sometimes the neighbours having intercourse. I visualise the pain and agony of the modern human being and sometimes bad Soviet films. Herewith I present you with my latest poem collection. It is a collection of poems. One day every literature book in Zanzibar will record my name so make sure you get it right.
