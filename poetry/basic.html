Basic Arithmetic
It took me 1 year to get to that place,
and another 2 years to return.

It took another 3 years to realize I had to go back
and by the time I was finished 4 years had passed.

5 years later nothing was as hard as it was at first,
but it was only by year 6 that I remembered I was telling this story to my dog
and had to multiply everything by 7 years.
