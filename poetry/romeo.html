Romeo and Juliet
In the sky I wrote her name.
Then I wrote mine.
I compared.
I liked mine better.
We broke up.

Playing with the sea water she asked me to write 'I love you' on the sand.
I said no.
She did not like the idea.
'Look a starfish', I said.
She flipped me off.

She saw some flowers she liked and she asked me to cut one for her.
I said I would be damned for eternity if I did it because some bee could have used that flower one day.
We did not have sex that night.

I had some scrap paper lying on my desk.
She gave me one blank sheet and told me to write a poem for her.
I sighed and wrote exacltly eleven words on it.
She said 'is this a poem?'
I said, 'It's symbolic and if you don't get it go fuck yourself'.
